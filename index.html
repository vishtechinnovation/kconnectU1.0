Our Story

FAQ

Frequenty asked questions

How do I book a service on KonnectU?

Simply download our app, choose the service you need, and get matched with a qualified provider near you.

Is it safe to pay through the app?

Yes. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted system for your peace of mind.

Can I choose the provider I want?

Yes. You’ll see profiles, ratings, and reviews to help you choose the right expert for your job.

How do I become a service provider?

Visit the “Join as a Service Provider” section or download our app to register and submit your documents.

How do I contact support?

If you need help with our platform or have any other questions, you can contact the kconnectU's support team by submitting a support request through the website or by emailing support@konnectusolutions.com.

What areas do you currently serve?

We’re based in Ontario and expanding quickly. Check the app for service availability in your area.

Can I reschedule or cancel a booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel via the app before the agreed service time. Please check our cancellation policy.
